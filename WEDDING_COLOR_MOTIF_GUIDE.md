# Wedding Color Motif Guide
## Best Use Cases for Wedding Style & Vibe

### Color Palette
**Full Motif Colors:** `["935F3B", "85441E", "38150B", "EFBE94", "B26A3B"]`
- `#935F3B` - Medium Brown (Warm, earthy)
- `#85441E` - Dark Brown (Rich, elegant)
- `#38150B` - Deep Brown/Black (Sophisticated accent)
- `#EFBE94` - Light Peach/Beige (Soft, romantic)
- `#B26A3B` - Rust Brown (Warm accent)

**Primary Colors (Used in Sections):**
- `#EFBE94` - Light Peach/Beige (Backgrounds, soft elements)
- `#85441E` - Dark Brown (Text, borders, decorative elements)

---

## Best Use Cases by Element

### 1. **Backgrounds & Base Colors**
**Best Use:** `#EFBE94` (Light Peach/Beige)
- **Why:** Creates a warm, romantic, and elegant foundation
- **Use Cases:**
  - Section backgrounds
  - Card backgrounds (with white overlay)
  - Soft gradient overlays
  - Creates a vintage, timeless wedding vibe

### 2. **Text & Typography**
**Best Use:** `#85441E` (Dark Brown)
- **Why:** Excellent readability with warm, sophisticated feel
- **Use Cases:**
  - Headings (Cinzel font for elegance)
  - Body text (Cormorant Garamond for readability)
  - Script text (WindSong for romantic accents)
  - Creates a classic, refined aesthetic

### 3. **Borders & Accents**
**Best Use:** `#85441E` with varying opacity
- **Why:** Defines elements without being harsh
- **Use Cases:**
  - Card borders (`border-[#85441E]/30` for subtle)
  - Hover states (`border-[#85441E]/50` for emphasis)
  - Decorative lines and dividers
  - QR code borders

### 4. **Buttons & Interactive Elements**
**Best Use:** 
- Primary: `#85441E` background with `#EFBE94` text
- Secondary: `#EFBE94` background with `#85441E` text/border
- **Why:** High contrast for accessibility, maintains elegance
- **Use Cases:**
  - "Get Directions" buttons (primary)
  - "Copy Address" buttons (secondary)
  - Hover states: Darker shade `#6d361b`

### 5. **Decorative Elements**
**Best Use:** `#85441E` for corner decorations
- **Why:** Creates elegant, non-intrusive framing
- **Use Cases:**
  - Corner decorations (using mask images)
  - Floral accents
  - Subtle background patterns
  - Creates a cohesive, polished look

### 6. **Color Palette Displays**
**Best Use:** All 5 motif colors together
- **Why:** Shows the full range of the wedding theme
- **Use Cases:**
  - Attire color palette displays
  - Theme color showcases
  - Visual guides for guests
  - Demonstrates the complete color story

### 7. **Cards & Containers**
**Best Use:** 
- Background: `#FFF7F6` (off-white) with `#85441E` borders
- Accent backgrounds: `#EFBE94/20` gradients
- **Why:** Clean, elegant contrast
- **Use Cases:**
  - Venue cards
  - Attire information cards
  - Reminder sections
  - Creates a premium, invitation-like feel

### 8. **Image Overlays**
**Best Use:** 
- Text: White with `#EFBE94` accents
- Overlay: Dark gradient (`from-black/70`)
- **Why:** Ensures text readability while maintaining elegance
- **Use Cases:**
  - Venue image overlays
  - Photo captions
  - Creates depth and sophistication

## Typography Pairing

### **Headings:** Cinzel
- Elegant, serif font
- Perfect for titles and section headers
- Complements the sophisticated color palette

### **Body Text:** Cormorant Garamond
- Readable, elegant serif
- Works beautifully for descriptions and details
- Maintains readability at all sizes

### **Script/Accents:** WindSong
- Romantic, flowing script
- Perfect for dates, names, and special moments
- Adds a personal, intimate touch

---

## Implementation Tips

1. **Consistency is Key:** Use `#EFBE94` and `#85441E` as your primary pair throughout
2. **Full Palette for Special Moments:** Use all 5 colors when showcasing the complete theme
3. **Opacity Variations:** Use opacity (e.g., `/20`, `/30`, `/50`) for subtle effects
4. **Contrast Matters:** Always ensure text is readable (dark text on light backgrounds)
5. **Hover States:** Darken colors slightly on hover for interactive feedback
6. **Decorative Elements:** Use corner decorations sparingly for elegant framing

---

## Color Psychology

- **Brown (`#85441E`)**: Stability, reliability, warmth, earthiness
- **Peach/Beige (`#EFBE94`)**: Softness, romance, elegance, comfort
- **Combined**: Creates a sense of grounded romance, timeless elegance, and warm sophistication
